WELCOME TO WILBERLAND

Wilberland institute is a Citadel of knowledge, an empire for the grooming of the intectuals. Our interest is in improving the lives and status of the country. Our commitment is to impact very positively in every Nigerian youth and to improve the economy and well-being of Nigeria by improving on our educational standard.

OUR MISSION

To produce graduates who are worthy in character and sound judgement. To expand the frontiers of knowledge through provision of excellent conditions for learning and research

OUR VISION

To be a word class institution for academic excellence geared towards meeting societal needs by inculcating technological ideas into our horizons of learning, imbueing our students with that unfathomable confidence to challenging whatever challenges they encounter in any part of the globe

EXECUTIVE PROGRAMMES

We are accredited by The Association of MBAs (AMBA). This puts WI amongst the exclusive group of only 2% of business Schools in 70 countries to achieve this accreditation

In 2016, WI became the first institution in West Africa to be accredited by the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACBS)

In 2014, WI joined the Global Network for Advanced Management (GNAM), an alliance of 32 leading business schools that seek to drive innovation and create valued collaboration.
